Quick Tips

  • Built-in free tracks come from openly licensed or public-domain sources.
  • Put songs in the project's music folder to auto-load them every time.
  • Use Add Folder to import an entire music folder at once.
  • Click any track to play it immediately.
  • Use the search box to filter songs by title or filename.
  • PulsePlay does not stream copyrighted music from the internet; it plays the audio files you import.
